 **“AI-Driven Stock Price Prediction Using LSTM & GRU”**, the **requirements** can be categorized into three main parts:

---

### 🔧 **1. Technical Requirements**

#### 📦 **Python Libraries / Dependencies**
Here’s a list of packages based on your code:
- `numpy` – numerical operations
- `pandas` – data manipulation
- `yfinance` – fetch stock market data
- `matplotlib`, `seaborn` – data visualization
- `tensorflow` – build and train LSTM & GRU models
- `scikit-learn (sklearn)` – scaling and preprocessing
- `joblib` / `pickle` – model serialization
- `PIL` – image processing (for UI)
- `streamlit` – interactive web app
- `base64`, `requests`, `os`, `time`, `warnings`, `matplotlib.dates` – additional utilities

#### 🧠 **Machine Learning Models**
- **LSTM** (Long Short-Term Memory)
- **GRU** (Gated Recurrent Units)

#### ⚙️ **Tools**
- **coolab** – for model development & testing
- **Streamlit** – to deploy and run the web app
- **VS code** – for managing environments (recommended)

---

### 💻 **System Requirements**
- RAM: 8GB minimum (for training models)
- GPU: Optional (faster training)
- Disk: At least 1GB free space
- OS: Windows/Linux/macOS
